# Gentle Monster Integrated Marketing Campaign

A full-scale integrated marketing campaign strategy for **Gentle Monster**, a luxury eyewear brand competing in the $100B+ global eyewear market. This case study demonstrates strategic campaign development, account planning, and measurable growth architecture.

## Strategic Challenge

How to position a luxury eyewear brand in a saturated market while driving customer acquisition and lifetime value in a competitive, price-sensitive segment—across multiple geographic regions and sales channels.

## Campaign Strategy & Framework

**Account Strategy**
- Competitive positioning analysis and brand differentiation
- Customer journey mapping across awareness → consideration → conversion
- Revenue model development (DTC vs. retail partnerships vs. wholesale)

**Integrated Marketing Approach**
- Omnichannel campaign architecture (social, influencer, experiential, retail)
- Audience segmentation with psychographic and behavioral targeting
- Message hierarchy and creative strategy aligned to conversion funnel
- Channel mix optimization with budget allocation rationale

**Measurable Outcomes Focus**
- KPI framework tied to business objectives (CAC, LTV, AOV, retention)
- Campaign attribution and performance tracking methodology
- Quarterly goal-setting with variance analysis and optimization loops

**Global Perspective**
- Market-specific strategy considerations (regional preferences, competitive landscape, regulatory environment)
- Localized messaging while maintaining brand consistency
- Cross-market learning and best practice application

## What's Included

- Strategic situation analysis and market opportunity
- Audience personas with behavioral and demographic depth
- Integrated media strategy with channel rationale and sequencing
- Creative direction, messaging pillars, and campaign narrative
- KPI dashboard and success metrics
- Implementation timeline and resource requirements

## Skills Demonstrated

- **Account Strategy**: Competitive positioning, customer segmentation, revenue model development
- **Integrated Marketing**: Campaign architecture, channel strategy, creative direction, audience targeting
- **Strategic Planning**: Market analysis, competitive differentiation, goal-setting, KPI development
- **Data-Driven Thinking**: Attribution modeling, performance metrics, optimization frameworks
- **Global Perspective**: Market research, localization strategy, cross-market insights
